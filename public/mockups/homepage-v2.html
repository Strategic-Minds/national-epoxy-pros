How It Works

The 6-Step Process

Every install follows the same professional sequence — from estimate to final walkthrough.

01

Sign Up & Schedule

Start your digital estimate and choose your finish direction. No commitment required.

02

Prep Work

Concrete is diamond-ground and patched for a correct bond profile.

03

Base Coat

The base coat is matched to your slab, use case, and broadcast system.

04

Beauty Coat

Flake, metallic, or quartz creates your visual finish and texture profile.

05

Topcoat Finish

Polyaspartic topcoat locks in the system for durability and sheen.

06

Final Inspection

Walkthrough, cure guidance, and care instructions. Job reviewed for quality.
